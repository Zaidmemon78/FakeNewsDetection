    About Fake News Detector

    Our mission is to help people identify misinformation and promote media literacy.

    Our Mission

    We aim to combat the spread of misinformation by providing tools that help people verify the credibility of news content they encounter online.

    Our Technology

    We use advanced AI and machine learning algorithms to analyze news content for signs of misinformation, bias, and factual inaccuracies.

    Our Team

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How accurate is the fake news detection?

    Our system has an accuracy rate of approximately 85-90% based on our testing. However, we always recommend verifying information from multiple sources.

    How does the detection algorithm work?

    Our algorithm analyzes various factors including language patterns, source credibility, cross-referencing with known facts, and comparison with verified news sources.

    Is my data secure when I use this tool?

    Yes, we do not store the full text of articles you submit. We only keep anonymized metadata for improving our algorithm.

    Can I use this tool for any language?

    Currently, our tool works best with English content. We are working on expanding to other languages in the future.
